5. Dominance:

6. FTT (Failure To Traverse):
Occurs AFTER Point 3 when price fails to reach prior extreme.

STRICT definition:
- Attempted continuation
- Fails to exceed prior P2/P3 boundary
- Followed by reversal confirmation

Only ONE FTT per container.

States:

S2: Non-Dominant Pullback
S3: Point 3 Formation
S4: Post-P3 Continuation Attempt
S5: FTT (Failure To Traverse)
S6: Reversal / New Sequence

S1 → S2  (volume shift begins)
S2 → S3  (pullback completes)
S3 → S4  (new push attempt)
S4 → S5  (fails to break prior extreme)
S5 → S6  (reversal confirmed)
S6 → S1  (new cycle)

Invariant:
There can only be ONE S5 (FTT) per container.
